A problem happening to many hearing aids and cochlear implant users

It’s not likely that any styles of earbuds and earphones are going to work with hearing aids.
The reason is quite straightforward: hearing aids already sit within ears, so fitting another device into the ears doesn't work.

The first earphone for all hearing conditions

We are launching a campaign for producing a brand new earphone design for you — no matter you are hearing normal, a hearing aid user, or a cochlear implant user.

Hearing technology for everyone

Rearia is the first earphone that is universal for hearing aid users, cochlear implant users, and hearing-normal people. Thanks to its modularized design with telecoil technology. You can wear it no matter if you wear both sides of aids, single-side, or just no aid. The telecoil technology is an old technology which provides high sound quality and compatible with most of the hearing aid and cochlear implant products.

We do everything that normal earphones do,
but even better.

This is not just a feel-good product. We want everyone to enjoy it.
