A common problem for many hearing aid and cochlear implant users


It’s not likely that any style of earbuds or earphones are going to work with hearing aids. The reason is quite straightforward — hearing aids already sit within ears, so fitting another device into the ears isn't possible.

⟶

The first earphone for all hearing conditions

We're launching a campaign to create a new, innovative earphone designed for you — whether you are normal hearing, a hearing aid user, or a cochlear implant user.

Universal and modular design

Rearia Hai is the first earphone that is universal for all hearing aid users, cochlear implant users, and hearing people. Thanks to its modular design with telecoil technology, you can wear it no matter whether you wear bilateral aids, single-sided, or just no aids at all. You can quickly and easily switch between the earbuds and coils and mix them as you want.

Experience superior sound quality

Telecoil technology is an established technology which is compatible with most hearing aids and cochlear implants. We developed this versatile technology further to provide outstanding sound quality for enjoying music.

Stop the buzz

For even further improved listening experience, we developed the first technology to cancel any bothering interference from nearby electrical infrastructure. We call it DBC (Dynamic Buzz Cancellation). It lets you enjoy music free from buzzing in difficult environments — such as in a plane or near power systems.

Intuitive and seamless usage

Convenient design for easy usage with three buttons operation — answer calls, change tracks and adjust volume easily. You can also activate your smartphone assistant for seamless integration with your smartphone.

Clear conversations

Call your loved ones, friends or business clients with the built-in microphone — integrated with technology which provides clear voice and low latency.

With you anywhere, anytime

Whatever the weather might be, you can bring Hai anywhere with you. Lightweight design allows it to easily clip to your clothes — or wear it like a necklace! Water resistant and modern design lets you wear it while exercising, working or just relaxing and enjoying music.
