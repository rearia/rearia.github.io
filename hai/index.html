Perfect solution for many hearing aid and cochlear implant users

It’s not likely that any style of earbuds or earphones are going to work with hearing aids. The reason is quite straightforward — hearing aids already sit within ears, so fitting another device into the ears isn't possible.

Universal and modular design

Rearia Hai is the first earphone that is universal for all hearing aid users, cochlear implant users, and hearing people. Thanks to its modular design with neckcoil technology, you can wear it no matter whether you wear bilateral aids, single-sided, or just no aids at all. You can quickly and easily switch between the earbuds and coils and mix them as you want.

Experience superior sound quality

Telecoil technology is an established technology which is compatible with most hearing aids and cochlear implants. We developed this versatile technology further to provide outstanding sound quality for enjoying music.

Stop the buzz

For even further improved listening experience, we developed the first technology to cancel any bothering interference from nearby electrical infrastructure. We call it DBC (Dynamic Buzz Cancellation). It lets you enjoy music free from buzzing in difficult environments — such as in a plane or near power systems.

Adjust with the App

With Hai app, we provide even more! Change your preferred equalizer settings and save them to suit all situations. Let speech stand out clearer when making a phone call. It also gives guidance to change the stereo balance of your phone to fit your hearing condition.

Intuitive and seamless usage

Convenient design for easy usage with three buttons operation — answer calls, change tracks and adjust volume easily. You can also talk to your Google Assistant or Siri with Hai for seamless integration with your smartphone.

Clear conversations

Call your loved ones, friends or business clients with the built-in microphone — integrated with technology which provides clear voice and low latency.

Express your style

Customize your Hai with different coloured surfaces and stickers! Let your creativity run free and let your Hai show your style.

With you anywhere, anytime

Whatever the weather might be, you can bring Hai anywhere with you. Lightweight design allows it to easily clip to your clothes — or simply wear it like a necklace! Water resistant and modern design lets you wear it while exercising, working or just relaxing and enjoying music.

How to use


⟶

① Connect
Connect Hai to any of your Bluetooth audio sources.

② Cancel the buzz
Hai's DBC technology clear the buzzing sound.

③ Enjoy
Hai's neckcoil send a high quality audio signal to your hearing aids or cochlear implants.

Key Features


⟶

Superb sound quality

Designed by Hi-Fi experts for maximum sound quality. Works with both hearing aids and cochlear implants with t-coil mode.

Dynamic Buzz Cancellation

Innovative technology reduces noise from electrical infrastructure nearby, a common problem for hearing aid users with telecoil.

Intuitive interface

Convenient usage with three buttons. Change volume and tracks, answer calls or wake up your smartphone assistant.

Modular and interchangeable coils and earbuds work for both hearing aids, cochlear implants and hearing people. Can also be used with single sided hearing aids.
