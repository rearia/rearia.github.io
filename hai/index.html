Universal compatibility and connectivity

Hai is compatible with all hearing aids and cochlear implants with T-mode. It can receive audio from all the Bluetooth sources and then send it to your devices.

Born for Flexible use

Hai lets you switch between earbuds and neckcoils to match the aids and ears. It beats most of the streamers failing to provide stereo sound to single-sided users or users of two different brands.

Experience superior sound quality

Unlike most of the streamers do not allow their users to enjoy stereo sound if they use single-sided aids or the aids by the different brands. We developed telecoil, an established and versatile technology, further to provide outstanding sound quality for the diverse aid usage scenarios.

How to use


⟶
①

Connect

Connect your Hai device to any of your Bluetooth audio sources (e.g. mobile phone).

②

Turning on T-mode

Turning on the T-mode of your aids. Hai's DBC technology clears the buzzing sound while using the t-mode.

③

Enjoy and be fashionable

Hai's neckcoil sends a high quality audio signal to your hearing aids and/or cochlear implants. All while looking good!

Switch made easy

If you use a single hearing aid, you can attach one Hai earbud for your hearing side, and use telecoil for the side with hearing aid. If you want to use Hai without your hearing aids and/or implants, just plug in two earbuds and modify the sound with our app.

We provide even more!


⟶

Adjust with the Hai App

Adjust with the Hai App. Change your preferred equalizer settings and save them to suit all situations. Let speech stand out clearer when making a phone call. It also gives guidance to change the stereo balance of your phone to fit your hearing condition.

Stop the buzz

For even further improved listening experience, we developed the first technology to cancel any bothering interference from nearby electrical infrastructure. We call it DBC (Dynamic Buzz Cancellation). It lets you enjoy music free from buzzing in difficult environments — such as in a plane or near power systems.

Clear conversations

Call your loved ones, friends, or business clients with the built-in microphone — integrated with technology which provides clear voice and low latency.

Intuitive and seamless usage

Convenient design for easy usage with three buttons operation — answer calls, change tracks, and adjust volume easily. You can also talk to your Google Assistant or Siri with Hai for seamless integration with your smartphone.

Express your style

Customize your Hai with different colored surfaces and stickers! Let your creativity run free and let your Hai show your style.

With you anywhere, anytime

Whatever the weather might be, you can bring Hai anywhere with you. Lightweight design allows it to easily clip to your clothes — or simply wear it like a necklace! Water-resistant and modern design lets you wear it while exercising, working, or just relaxing and enjoying music.

Key Features

High-quality microphone
