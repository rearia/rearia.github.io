    A problem happening to many hearing aids and cochlear implant users


    It’s not likely that any styles of earbuds and earphones are going to work with hearing aids. The reason is quite straightforward: hearing aids already sit within ears, so fitting another device into the ears doesn't work.

    The first earphone for all hearing conditions

    We are launching a campaign for producing a brand new earphone design for you — no matter you are hearing normal, a hearing aid user, or a cochlear implant user.

    Universal design

    Rearia HAI is the first earphone that is universal for hearing aid users, cochlear implant users, and hearing-normal people. Thanks to its modularized design with telecoil technology. You can wear it no matter if you wear both sides of aids, single-side, or just no aid. You can quickly and easily switch between earbuds and coils. You can even have one coil and one earbud in any configuration you need.

    Superior sound quality

    Telecoil technology is an old technology which provides high sound quality and is compatible with most of the hearing aid and cochlear implant products. We used this technology as basis and with our HiFi specialized team developed an outstanding sound quality for enjoying music

    Active Noise Cancellation

    To even further improve listening experience, we integrated Active Noise Cancellation to block any unwanted interference from electrical infrastructure. This allows the listener to hear great sound quality even in difficult environments, such as in a plane or near high-voltage systems.

    Adjustable equalizer

    With HAI App, we provide even more! Change your preferred equalizer settings and save them to suit all listening situations. You can also change the stereo balance to fit HAI with your hearing condition. Connect with others, you can also share your hearing profile with other users similar to you.

    Intuitive and seamless usage

    Convenient design allows for easy usage with three buttons operation; Answer calls, change tracks, adjust volume easily. You can also activate your smartphone assistant. Seamless integration with your smartphone.

    Clear conversations

    Call to your loved ones with the built-in microphone which provides clear voice and low latency.

    Express your style

    Choose either a black or white device and customize your product with different coloured surfaces and stickers! Let your creativity run free and let your device express your style.

    With you anywere, anytime

    Whether the sun is shining, or its raining outside, you can bring HAI anywhere with you. Its lightweight design allows it to easily clip to your clothes, or wear it like a necklace! Water resistant and modern design lets you wear it while exercising, working or just relaxing and enjoying the music
